# Incident RCA Playbook

## Inputs
- Incident record (runtime/ops/incidents)
- Timeline events (runtime/ops/timeline)
- Audit ledger entries
- Evidence pack

## Steps
1. Collect evidence pack.
2. Identify timeline start, mitigation steps, and recovery point.
3. Correlate actions with audit entries.
4. Record root cause and contributing factors.
5. Propose corrective actions and follow-up runbooks.

## Outputs
- RCA summary
- Action items with owners and due dates
